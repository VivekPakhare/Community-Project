Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register on CampusCart, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted), college name, and role (buyer/seller).
  • Profile Information: Avatar, bio, phone number, and verification status.
  • Product Listings: Product details, images, pricing, location (city, area, pin code), and contact information you provide.
  • Transaction Data: Order history, payment method preferences, and communication between buyers and sellers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, and product views to improve your experience.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the CampusCart platform.
  • Facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers.
  • Enable messaging and communication features.
  • Send important notifications about your orders and account.
  • Ensure platform safety and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Display relevant products and categories based on your preferences.
3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information may be shared only in these cases:

  • With other users: Your public profile, seller contact details, and product listings are visible to other registered users.
  • With your consent: When you choose to enable call or chat features, your contact information is shared with potential buyers.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted password storage and secure HTTPS connections. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information through your profile.
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting support.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
6. Cookies

CampusCart uses cookies to maintain your login session and improve performance. These are essential cookies required for the platform to function properly.
